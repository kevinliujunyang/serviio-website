Frequently Asked Questions

Everything restaurant owners ask before getting started.

Does Serviio work with my POS system?
Yes — we integrate directly with Square, Toast, Clover, Chowbus, Mealkeyway, and any POS that exposes an API. Orders flow straight from the phone call to your kitchen display with no manual entry needed.
Will the AI sound robotic to my customers?
No. Serviio uses natural conversational AI that speaks fluently, handles interruptions, and adjusts to local accents. Most customers don't realize they're talking to an AI unless you tell them.
What happens if the AI doesn't understand a customer?
When the AI is uncertain, it asks clarifying questions just like a human would. For complex situations, it can transfer the call to a staff member or take a callback message. We monitor unclear interactions and continuously improve accuracy.
Can I keep my existing restaurant phone number?
Yes. We forward your existing number to Serviio so customers see no change. If you'd rather get a new number, we'll provision one for you. Either way, the setup takes minutes.
Are there contracts or cancellation fees?
No contracts, no cancellation fees. You only pay 2% on completed orders, and you can cancel anytime. We'll forward your number back to your old line within 24 hours.
How is customer data handled?
Call recordings and order data are encrypted in transit and at rest. We don't share customer information with third parties. You retain full ownership of your customer data and can export or delete it at any time.
