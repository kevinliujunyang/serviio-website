## What is Serviio?
AI-powered voice agent that automates restaurant phone orders. Answers calls 24/7, takes orders through natural conversation, and sends them to the kitchen.

## Key Features
- 7 languages: English, Spanish, Chinese, Japanese, Hindi, French, Italian
- Multi-line capability (handle multiple calls simultaneously)
- Direct POS integration with existing systems
- SMS order confirmations
- 24/7 availability

## Pricing
- **2% per completed order**
- No monthly fees, no setup costs

## Tagline
"Your team serves guests. Every call answered by us. Zero missed."
